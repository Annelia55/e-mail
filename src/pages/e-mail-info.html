Bulgari Art Award

The Bulgari Art Award is an annual award to support contemporary Australian painting. Each year, the Gallery identifies a significant painting by a mid-career Australian artist to be acquired for the collection. The award, sponsored by Bulgari, consists of $50,000 for acquisition of the painting and a residency for the artist in Italy valued at $30,000. The total award of $80,000 makes it one of the most valuable art awards in Australia.

The Art Gallery of NSW awards the Dyason Bequest to an Australian art student who has already won a travelling scholarship, to assist with the cost of overseas travel. This enables the recipient to better study art in countries other than Australia.

Second title

Each year, the Brett Whiteley Studio, administered by the Art Gallery of NSW, invites applications for a major travelling art scholarship. The scholarship is awarded to a talented young artist/painter with an established body of work who is best able to demonstrate the use and benefit of the scholarship to further their art education in Europe.

You can apply till 22-nd of August. Further details available on the Brett Whiteley Studio pages.
